Our Services

Osmosis provides a full spectrum of composite services — from concept to delivery.

From Sketch to Design
We transform our clients' ideas into reality, starting with simple sketches or concepts and culminating in 3D models and technical CAD designs. We carefully monitor each stage to ensure the final result fully reflects the initial vision.
Fiberglass Models and Molds
In addition to producing finished components, we create fiberglass (FRP) models and molds of varying sizes and complexities, ensuring precision and quality at every stage of the process.
Integration of Additional Components
The production of some composite products requires the integration of elements such as sheet metal, plates, shells, or other metal reinforcements. We offer complete products with all these components thanks to our collaboration with companies specializing in metal, wood, and other materials. Processing includes laser cutting, milling, and other precision techniques, allowing us to provide complete and perfectly integrated solutions.
Bodywork Restoration
We perform restoration work on fiberglass bodies, repairing and refinishing damaged surfaces to restore them to their original condition, both aesthetically and functionally.

About Us

Osmosis specializes in creative composite design and production, blending innovation, functionality, and aesthetics. With a passion for form and precision, Osmosis transforms ideas into durable, high-quality creations that inspire imagination and elevate experiences.
